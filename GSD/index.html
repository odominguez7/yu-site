§ 02 / The New Thesis

Wearables drowned people in data.
YU returns one decision.

i.

The problem

People wear two or three devices that each tell a different story, and none of them answer the only question that matters in the morning.

ii.

The opening

YU consolidates signals across every device a person wears and returns one clear action each morning, interpreted manually now and automated next.

iii.

The founder fit

The founder spent a decade recovering idle capacity inside a manufacturing business and rebuilt his own body through the same disciplined measurement YU now sells to others.

§ 04 / The Persona

Meet the user we already know by name.

Primary user · Marcus Chen

32, Austin.Three wearables, zero answers.

Senior Product Manager at a mid stage B2B SaaS company. Trains five to seven sessions a week across CrossFit, running, and HYROX prep. Engaged, wedding in fourteen months. Wears Oura, Whoop, and Garmin simultaneously and still cannot get a clear answer in the morning.

$140K
Household income, no kids
$336
Monthly spend on wearables, supplements, coaching
8×
Wearable check ins per day across three apps
The money is already on the table

He pays $30 a month for Whoop, $6 for Oura, runs a $100 plus monthly supplement stack, and used to pay a remote coach $200 a month for exactly the synthesis YU now offers. High willingness to pay is not a hypothesis, it is his existing spend.

The pain is daily and specific

Oura says rest. Whoop says push. Garmin says something else. He stands at the kitchen counter doing math in his head, pastes screenshots into ChatGPT for generic answers, and starts the day already depleted by the interpretation itself.

The fear underneath

Marcus is terrified of becoming an average person. His self worth is welded to the data telling him he is still winning, and every contradictory reading is a small identity crisis. YU is not a wellness app for him. It is permission to rest without feeling like he has given up.

§ 06 / The 90 Day Plan

June 1 to August 30. The path to first revenue.

Graduation is not a finish line, it is the starting line. OPT activates on June 1 and the delta v decision lands the same week. This is the plan from that day forward, thirteen weeks, three months, one goal: turn YU into a venture with paying users and a defensible pre seed story.

Month 01 · Activation
June
Jun 1 to Jun 30 · Weeks 1 to 4

Stand up delta v or stand up the equivalent.

The first week resets identity from student to founder. The next three build the v1 that replaces the manual v0 from GSD.

    Week 1 · Jun 1 to 7delta v onboarding if accepted, or self run summer sprint from the Martin Trust Center if not. Full time cadence established.
  • 02
